📲 Introducing ScanCraft

ScanCraft is a lightweight, secure, and scalable humanitarian aid platform designed for conflict zones like Chad. It empowers relief workers and civilians alike by turning technology into survival tools.

🛠️ Smart Survival Assistance

One of the core features of ScanCraft is its AI-powered scanning system. By scanning items available to the user—such as scrap metal, cloth, batteries, or containers—ScanCraft intelligently suggests and guides how to combine them into usable tools or survival items.

From purifying water to crafting simple cooking setups or even emergency signaling devices, ScanCraft teaches survival through what you already have.

🔍 Key Features:

  • 🛰️ Real-time conflict and territory mapping
  • 📦 Inventory scanning and resource logging
  • 🤖 AI crafting guide based on scanned items
  • 📋 Survivor ID registration & local coordination
  • 🔐 Encrypted communication for field teams
